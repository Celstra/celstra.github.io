Artist Based Web Apps

Digital Art Director

Based on the THUMB WAR : Design Iteration Combat Simulation by Paul Richards. These are real prompts that you would see while working in a studio and an art director would come to you and say "Hey! give me this or that." So I went ahead and designed a really simple web app to help in making thumbnails followed by revisions.

Creature Generator

I built out this creature generator for Tressa Geniets who runs a Facebook Group called 'Wildlife and Creature Design Studies and Prompts'. She is super cool and recently launched a website where you can find the generator that I created matching the style of her website.

Board Game Apps

Oathsworn: Into the Deepwood Might Deck Manager

I kickstarted oathsworn and wanted to make a might deck manager since the decks are quite small and can be hard to shuffle. I am also thinking of ways to improve this one as well. Let me know if there is something you want to see added.

Kingdomdeath Monster Simple Dice Roller

Really quick and simple dice roller for Kingdom Death Monster board game. Was trying out some cool things and I plan on expanding a little more to see what I can really do with it. [COMING SOON]
